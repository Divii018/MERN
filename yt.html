A bicycle is a human-powered vehicle with two wheels aligned in a straight line, designed primarily for transportation, recreation, and fitness. It is propelled by pedaling, which turns the chain and drives the rear wheel. Bicycles are simple, efficient, and environmentally friendly, making them one of the most widely used forms of transport around the world. They come in various types, including road bikes, mountain bikes, hybrid bikes, and electric bikes (e-bikes), each suited to different terrains and purposes.
